Before to go

Before to close GIMP is a very good practice to do:

  1. Save session (Ctrl+S in Teseo2 context)
  2. Close Teseo2.
  3. Save xcf image, it contains all your paths and layers. xcf is your friend and gzip or bzip2 too...
  4. Close GIMP.
